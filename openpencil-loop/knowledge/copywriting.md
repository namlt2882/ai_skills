# Copywriting Rules

## Text Length
- Headlines: 2-6 words
- Subtitles: 1 sentence, max 15 words
- Feature titles: 2-4 words
- Descriptions: 1 sentence, max 20 words
- Buttons: 1-3 words
- Card text: max 2 sentences
- Stats: number + 1-3 word label

## Style
- NEVER 3+ sentence paragraphs
- Distill to essence
- Power words > vague adjectives
