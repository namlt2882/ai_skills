# Design Principles

## Typography
- Type scale with real contrast: display 48-64, heading 28-36, body 16
- Weight: 700 titles, 500 subtitles, 400 body
- Line height: tighter at large sizes (1.05-1.15 for 40px+), looser at small (1.5-1.6 for 16px)

## Color
- Palette: 1 primary action color, 1 accent, neutral scale
- Max 2 saturated colors
- Page bg slightly tinted (#F8FAFC not #FFFFFF)
- WCAG AA contrast (4.5:1 body, 3:1 large text)

## Spacing
- 8px grid: related 8-16px, groups 24-32px, sections 48-80px
- Section padding: 80-120px vertical

## Layout
- Hero: ONE headline, ONE subtitle, ONE CTA
- Cards: consistent cornerRadius/padding/shadow
- Nav: logo + 3-5 links + CTA
- Alternate section backgrounds (white/#F8FAFC)
