# Market Analysis Aspect

This aspect covers both technical and fundamental analysis methodologies in trading.

## Technical Analysis Components
- Price action patterns
- Indicator analysis (RSI, MACD, Moving Averages)
- Chart patterns and formations
- Volume analysis
- Support and resistance levels

## Fundamental Analysis Components
- Economic indicators
- Company financials
- Market sentiment
- News impact assessment
- Sector rotation patterns

## Connection Points
- Links to Risk Management for position sizing based on analysis confidence
- Connects to Execution Methods for timing considerations
- Feeds into Portfolio Management for diversification decisions
