# US Financial Market Analysis Skill

A comprehensive AI skill for analyzing US financial markets with focus on intermarket relationships, risk assets correlation, technical indicators, and macroeconomic factors.

## Overview

This skill provides structured analysis of US financial markets emphasizing:
- **Intermarket relationships** between stocks, bonds, commodities, and currencies
- **Risk assets correlation** and safe haven analysis
- **Technical indicator interpretation** and market regime assessment
- **Macroeconomic factor evaluation** and event analysis

## Key Features

### Intermarket Analysis
- Analysis of the four pillars: stocks, bonds, commodities, currencies
- Leading vs lagging asset identification
- Correlation matrix monitoring
- Business cycle applications

### Risk Assets Assessment
- Risk-on/risk-off regime identification
- Safe haven vs risk asset performance
- Flight to quality pattern recognition
- Volatility regime analysis

### Technical Analysis
- Moving averages and trend identification
- Oscillators and momentum indicators
- Volume analysis and market internals
- Support/resistance and market structure

### Macroeconomic Analysis
- Federal Reserve policy impact assessment
- Economic indicator interpretation
- Global economic factor integration
- Event-driven market reaction analysis

## Usage

This skill is triggered when analyzing:
- US stock market performance and sector rotation
- Cross-asset correlations and intermarket relationships
- Risk sentiment and safe haven flows
- Technical patterns and market regime changes
- Macroeconomic data releases and policy announcements
- Currency and commodity market impacts

## Methodology

The skill follows a structured approach:
1. Intermarket analysis examining relationships between asset classes
2. Risk assets assessment identifying current regime
3. US stock market analysis focusing on leadership and breadth
4. Technical indicator interpretation for timing and confirmation
5. Macroeconomic environment evaluation
6. Risk assessment across time horizons
7. Actionable recommendations based on analysis

- Portfolio managers seeking comprehensive market analysis
- Traders looking for intermarket relationships
