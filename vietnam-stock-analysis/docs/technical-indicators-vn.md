# Vietnamese Technical Indicators Guide

Comprehensive guide to technical indicators specifically adapted for Vietnamese market characteristics and conditions.

## Market-Specific Considerations

### Price Limit Effects
- **Daily Limits**: 7% for most stocks, 10% for certain categories
- **Impact on Patterns**: Traditional patterns may be distorted by limits
- **Volume Spikes**: High volume when hitting daily limits
- **Post-Limit Behavior**: How stocks behave after hitting daily limits

### Liquidity Characteristics
- **Lower Average Volume**: Adjust expectations for indicator reliability
- **Concentrated Trading**: Higher volume in fewer stocks (VN30)
- **Session Patterns**: Different behavior in morning vs afternoon sessions
- **Foreign Flow Impact**: Large orders can significantly affect prices

### Market Structure
- **Retail Dominance**: Different behavioral patterns than institutional markets
- **Seasonal Patterns**: Vietnamese holiday and cultural event impacts
- **Regional Correlations**: Relationship with ASEAN and Asian markets
- **Global Sensitivity**: Response to international market movements

## Indicator Adaptations

### Moving Averages
- **Timeframe Adjustments**: Consider longer timeframes due to noise
- **Multiple MA Analysis**: Use confluence of multiple MAs for signals
- **Price Limit Adjustments**: Account for limit-induced price distortions
- **Volume Confirmation**: Require volume confirmation for MA signals

### Oscillators
- **RSI Adjustments**: May stay overbought/oversold longer due to momentum
- **Stochastic Modifications**: Consider market-specific overbought/oversold levels
- **MACD Settings**: Potentially adjust settings for market volatility
- **Momentum Divergence**: More significant in less efficient markets

### Volatility Indicators
- **Bollinger Bands**: Wider bands may be needed for Vietnamese volatility
- **ATR Multipliers**: Adjust stop-loss distances based on ATR
- **Keltner Channels**: Alternative to Bollinger Bands for trending markets
- **Donchian Channels**: For breakout strategies in Vietnamese market

## Trend Following Indicators

### Moving Average Systems
- **Dual MA Crossover**: Use 20-day and 50-day for shorter-term signals
- **Triple MA System**: 20-day, 50-day, and 200-day for trend confirmation
- **EMA vs SMA**: EMA may respond faster in trending conditions
- **MA Ribbon**: Multiple MAs to identify trend strength and direction

### Trend Following Tools
- **ADX (Average Directional Index)**: Measure trend strength in Vietnamese market
- **Parabolic SAR**: Dynamic stops for trend following strategies
- **Ichimoku Cloud**: Comprehensive trend analysis adapted for local market
- **Trend Lines**: Manual trend lines with volume confirmation
