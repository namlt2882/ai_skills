# Vietnamese Market Overview

Comprehensive overview of the Vietnamese stock market structure, participants, and operational characteristics.

## Market Structure

### Main Exchanges
- **HOSE (Ho Chi Minh Stock Exchange)**: Largest exchange by market capitalization and trading volume
  - Location: Ho Chi Minh City
  - Established: 2000
  - Listings: Over 350 companies
  - Focus: Large-cap and mid-cap companies
  - Index: VN-Index (capitalization-weighted)

- **HNX (Hanoi Stock Exchange)**: Secondary exchange with focus on mid-cap and smaller companies
  - Location: Hanoi
  - Established: 2005
  - Listings: Over 350 companies
  - Focus: Mid-cap, small-cap, and government bonds
  - Indices: HNX-Index, HNX30, HNX-Conect

- **UPCoM (Unlisted Public Company Market)**: Platform for unlisted public companies
  - Operated by: Vietnam Securities Depository (VSD)
  - Listings: Over 700 companies
  - Focus: Smaller companies, state-owned enterprise privatization
  - Characteristics: Lower liquidity than HOSE/HNX

### Market Segments
- **Equities**: Common stocks of listed companies
- **Preferred Shares**: Special voting or dividend rights
- **Bonds**: Government and corporate debt securities
- **ETFs**: Exchange-traded funds tracking various indices
- **Derivatives**: Futures contracts on major indices (limited offering)

## Trading Infrastructure

### Trading System
- **Electronic Platform**: Automated order matching system
- **Order Types**: Market orders, limit orders, at opening/closing auctions
- **Matching Algorithm**: Price-time priority principle
- **Trading Sessions**: Morning (9:00-11:30) and afternoon (1:00-3:00)
- **Opening/Closing**: 15-minute auction periods at start/end of session

### Settlement System
- **Settlement Cycle**: T+2 (trades settle 2 business days after trade date)
- **Clearing House**: Vietnam Securities Depository (VSD)
- **Delivery vs Payment**: Ensured settlement through depository system
- **Corporate Actions**: Automatic processing of dividends, splits, etc.

### Market Data Providers
- **Real-time Data**: Provided by exchanges and licensed vendors
- **Delayed Data**: Free and paid services available
- **Historical Data**: Available through various providers
- **Corporate Information**: Disclosed through official channels

## Market Participants

### Domestic Investors
- **Retail Investors**: Individual investors comprising majority of trading volume
- **Institutional Investors**: Pension funds, insurance companies, mutual funds
- **Securities Companies**: Brokerage firms providing trading services
- **Commercial Banks**: Investment in securities as part of portfolio

### Foreign Investors
- **Qualified Foreign Investors (QFIs)**: Licensed to invest in Vietnamese securities
- **Portfolio Investment**: Equity and bond investments
- **Direct Investment**: Foreign direct investment in listed companies
- **Constraints**: Subject to foreign ownership limits and regulations

### Government Entities
- **State Securities Commission (SSC)**: Primary regulator
- **State Bank of Vietnam (SBV)**: Monetary policy influence
- **Ministry of Finance (MOF)**: Policy oversight
- **State-Owned Enterprises**: Major issuers of securities

## Market Characteristics

### Trading Patterns
- **High Retail Participation**: Individual investors dominate trading activity
- **Volatility**: Higher volatility compared to developed markets
- **Liquidity Concentration**: High liquidity in top stocks, low in others
- **Seasonal Patterns**: Holiday and cultural event influences

### Price Formation
- **Price Limits**: Daily limits of 7% for most stocks, 10% for certain categories
- **Order Book Depth**: Limited depth compared to developed markets
- **Spread Characteristics**: Wider spreads for less liquid stocks
- **Market Making**: Limited formal market maker system

### Market Efficiency
- **Information Dissemination**: Improving but still lags developed markets
- **Price Discovery**: Active but influenced by retail sentiment
- **Arbitrage Opportunities**: More frequent than developed markets
- **Institutional Development**: Ongoing improvement in market sophistication

## Key Indices

### VN-Index (HOSE)
- **Composition**: Top companies by market capitalization on HOSE
- **Weighting**: Float-adjusted market capitalization
- **Base Value**: 100 points as of July 28, 2000
- **Review Frequency**: Quarterly review and rebalancing

### HNX-Index (HNX)
- **Composition**: Companies listed on HNX
- **Weighting**: Market capitalization
- **Base Value**: 100 points as of February 11, 2005
- **Characteristics**: Includes smaller companies than VN-Index

### UPCoM-Index (UPCoM)
- **Composition**: Companies on UPCoM market
- **Weighting**: Market capitalization
- **Characteristics**: Generally lower liquidity and capitalization

### Sector Indices
- **Banking Index**: Banking sector companies
- **Industrial Index**: Manufacturing and industrial companies
- **Consumer Goods Index**: Consumer-focused companies
- **Construction Index**: Construction and real estate companies

## Trading Considerations

### Market Hours
- **Trading**: Monday-Friday, 9:00 AM - 11:30 AM and 1:00 PM - 3:00 PM
- **Holidays**: Vietnamese holidays and weekends
- **Special Sessions**: Occasional extended or shortened sessions
- **Global Correlation**: Moderate correlation with regional markets

### Costs and Fees
- **Brokerage Commissions**: Typically 0.15-0.30% of transaction value
- **Government Fees**: Small percentage-based fees
- **Deposit/Custody**: Annual fees for holding securities
- **Currency Conversion**: FX costs for foreign investors

### Taxation
- **Capital Gains**: Tax on profits from securities transactions
- **Dividends**: Tax on dividend income
- **Withholding**: Automatic withholding by brokers/depositories
- **Residency**: Different rates for residents vs non-residents
