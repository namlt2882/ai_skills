# Vietnamese Regulatory Compliance

Detailed instructions for ensuring all trading recommendations and analysis comply with Vietnamese securities regulations and exchange requirements.

## Purpose
Ensure all trading activities, recommendations, and communications adhere to Vietnamese securities laws, regulations, and exchange requirements.

## Vietnamese Securities Laws

### Securities Law Framework
- **Law on Securities (2019)**: Primary legislation governing securities market
- **Law on Enterprises (2020)**: Corporate governance requirements
- **Law on Investment (2020)**: Foreign investment regulations
- **Law on Credit Institutions (2017)**: Banking sector regulations

### Key Regulatory Bodies
- **State Securities Commission of Vietnam (SSC)**: Primary securities regulator
- **Ministry of Finance (MOF)**: Policy oversight and coordination
- **State Bank of Vietnam (SBV)**: Monetary policy and banking oversight
- **Vietnam Securities Depository (VSD)**: Securities depository and settlement

## Exchange-Specific Rules

### HOSE (Ho Chi Minh Stock Exchange)
- **Listing Requirements**: Minimum capital, profitability, and governance standards
- **Trading Sessions**: 9:00-11:30 AM and 1:00-3:00 PM (excluding lunch break)
- **Price Limits**: 7% daily limit for most stocks, 10% for certain categories
- **Lot Sizes**: Standard 100-share lots with odd-lot provisions

### HNX (Hanoi Stock Exchange)
- **Trading Mechanisms**: Similar to HOSE with some differences in listing standards
- **Derivatives Market**: Futures contracts on VN30 index
- **Trading Hours**: Same as HOSE
- **Price Limits**: Same as HOSE

### UPCoM (Unlisted Public Company Market)
- **Less Stringent Requirements**: Lower listing standards than HOSE/HNX
- **Price Limits**: Generally same as HOSE/HNX
- **Trading Hours**: Same as HOSE/HNX
- **Liquidity Differences**: Typically lower liquidity than main exchanges

## Foreign Investment Regulations

### Foreign Ownership Limits (FOL)
- **General Limit**: 49% of charter capital for most companies
- **Individual Foreign Investors**: Maximum 5% ownership
- **Total Foreign Ownership**: Maximum 49% of total shares outstanding
- **Strategic Investors**: Potential for higher limits in specific cases

### Foreign Trading Permissions
- **Eligible Securities**: Listed stocks, bonds, and fund certificates
- **Trading Channels**: Through licensed securities companies
- **Settlement Requirements**: T+2 settlement system
- **Reporting Obligations**: Transaction reporting to authorities

### Foreign Investment Prohibited/Restricted Sectors
- **Prohibited Activities**: Certain defense/security-related businesses
- **Restricted Sectors**: Media, telecommunications, banking (foreign limits apply)
- **Conditional Access**: Various business lines with specific requirements

## Margin Trading Rules

### Eligibility Requirements
- **Account Age**: Minimum account maintenance period
- **Capital Requirements**: Minimum account balance for margin eligibility
- **Credit Assessment**: Financial capability evaluation
- **Knowledge Testing**: Understanding of margin trading risks

### Margin Requirements
- **Initial Margin**: Minimum percentage for opening positions
- **Maintenance Margin**: Minimum level to maintain positions
- **Mark-to-Market**: Daily valuation and margin calls
- **Collateral Types**: Acceptable securities for margin purposes

### Risk Management
- **Position Limits**: Maximum leverage ratios
- **Automatic Liquidation**: Forced selling when margin falls below threshold
- **Interest Rates**: Variable rates charged on borrowed funds
- **Monitoring Requirements**: Continuous account surveillance

## Disclosure and Reporting Requirements

### Corporate Disclosure
- **Quarterly Reports**: Financial statements and business updates
- **Annual Reports**: Comprehensive annual performance and outlook
- **Material Events**: Immediate disclosure of significant corporate events
- **Insider Information**: Prohibition on trading with material non-public information

### Transaction Reporting
- **Large Shareholding**: Notification when exceeding ownership thresholds
- **Related Party Transactions**: Disclosure of conflicts of interest
- **Short Selling**: Prohibition on naked short selling
- **Market Making**: Special rules for designated market makers

## Compliance Procedures

### Pre-Trade Compliance
- **Eligibility Verification**: Confirm trading permissions for clients
- **Position Limits**: Check against regulatory position limits
- **Account Status**: Verify account is in good standing
- **Product Suitability**: Ensure product suitability for client profile

### Trade Execution Compliance
- **Fair Execution**: Best execution obligations for client orders
- **Price Priority**: Time-price priority in order matching
- **Size Priority**: Proper handling of large orders
- **Market Impact**: Minimize market disruption from large trades

### Post-Trade Compliance
- **Settlement Processing**: Ensure timely settlement of trades
- **Record Keeping**: Maintain accurate transaction records
- **Reporting**: Submit required reports to regulators
- **Reconciliation**: Verify trade accuracy and settlement

## Risk Management Requirements

### Capital Adequacy
- **Minimum Capital**: Maintain required regulatory capital levels
- **Risk-Based Capital**: Capital requirements based on risk exposure
- **Liquidity Requirements**: Maintain sufficient liquid assets
- **Concentration Limits**: Limits on exposure to single counterparties

### Operational Risk
- **System Reliability**: Ensure trading system integrity
- **Data Security**: Protect client information and trading data
- **Business Continuity**: Plans for system failures or disasters
- **Audit Trails**: Maintain comprehensive audit trails

## Market Conduct Rules

### Prohibited Activities
- **Market Manipulation**: Artificially inflating or depressing prices
- **Insider Trading**: Trading on material non-public information
- **Fraudulent Practices**: Misleading clients or market participants
- **Abusive Trading**: Excessive order cancellation or wash trading

### Fair Dealing
- **Best Execution**: Execute client orders in their best interests
- **Priority Handling**: Proper prioritization of client vs proprietary orders
- **Information Barriers**: Prevent misuse of confidential information
- **Transparency**: Clear disclosure of trading arrangements

## Compliance Monitoring

### Surveillance Systems
- **Automated Monitoring**: Systems to detect suspicious trading patterns
- **Manual Review**: Human oversight of flagged transactions
- **Pattern Recognition**: Identify potential market manipulation
- **Cross-Market Monitoring**: Monitor across different exchanges

### Regular Reviews
- **Compliance Audits**: Internal and external compliance assessments
- **Policy Updates**: Regular review and update of compliance policies
- **Staff Training**: Ongoing education on regulatory requirements
- **Client Education**: Inform clients of their rights and obligations

## Integration with Trading Recommendations

### Recommendation Compliance
- **Suitability Assessment**: Ensure recommendations match client profile
- **Risk Disclosure**: Include appropriate risk warnings
- **Regulatory Restrictions**: Account for investment restrictions
- **Documentation**: Maintain records of recommendation rationale

### Client Communication
- **Clear Disclosure**: Transparent communication of terms and conditions
- **Regular Updates**: Keep clients informed of regulatory changes
- **Educational Content**: Help clients understand their obligations
- **Feedback Mechanisms**: Allow clients to report concerns

## Quality Standards

- Stay current with evolving Vietnamese securities regulations
- Implement robust compliance systems and procedures
- Maintain detailed records of all compliance activities
- Provide regular training to staff on regulatory requirements
- Conduct periodic compliance audits and assessments
- Ensure all recommendations consider regulatory constraints
- Maintain independence and objectivity in analysis
- Protect client confidentiality and privacy
- Promote fair and transparent market practices
