# Vietnamese Trading Recommendations

Detailed instructions for generating actionable trading recommendations for Vietnamese equities, incorporating technical, fundamental, and risk considerations.

## Purpose
Provide clear, actionable trading recommendations for Vietnamese stocks that align with market conditions, risk parameters, and user objectives.

## Recommendation Framework

### Entry Criteria
- **Technical Setup**: Clear technical pattern or indicator signal
- **Fundamental Alignment**: Company prospects supporting technical signal
- **Risk-Reward Ratio**: Favorable reward potential versus risk
- **Market Context**: Alignment with overall market regime
- **Liquidity Check**: Adequate trading volume for entry/exit

### Position Sizing
- **Account Percentage Risk**: Risk no more than 1-2% of account per trade
- **Volatility Adjustment**: Smaller sizes for higher volatility stocks
- **Correlation Consideration**: Reduce size when correlating with existing positions
- **Market Condition Adjustment**: Conservative sizing during uncertain periods
- **Confidence-Based Sizing**: Larger positions for higher conviction setups

### Timing Considerations
- **Market Hours**: Optimal entry timing within Vietnamese trading sessions
- **Volume Patterns**: Enter during higher volume periods for liquidity
- **News Avoidance**: Avoid positions before major news events
- **Technical Confluence**: Multiple indicator alignment for entry
- **Market Sentiment**: Overall market tone supporting individual stock

## Recommendation Types

### Long Positions
- **Momentum Plays**: Stocks showing strong upward technical momentum
- **Value Opportunities**: Undervalued stocks with improving fundamentals
- **Breakout Setups**: Stocks breaking above resistance with volume
- **Pullback Entries**: Buying dips in established uptrends
- **Earnings Plays**: Pre/post-earnings positions with defined risk

### Short Positions
- **Momentum Shorts**: Stocks showing strong downward technical momentum
- **Overvaluation Plays**: Expensively valued stocks with deteriorating fundamentals
- **Breakdown Setups**: Stocks breaking below support with volume
- **Earnings Shorts**: Pre/post-earnings positions with defined risk
- **Sector Rotation**: Betting against out of favor sectors

### Neutral/Hold Recommendations
- **Wait for Clarity**: Unclear technical picture requiring patience
- **Risk/Reward Unfavorable**: Poor risk-reward ratio for entry
- **High Uncertainty**: Upcoming events creating excessive risk
- **Market Conditions**: Broader market conditions not supportive
- **Better Opportunities**: Capital allocation to superior opportunities

## Vietnamese Market Specifics

### Price Limit Considerations
- **Daily Limits**: Account for 7% (most stocks) or 10% (special cases) daily limits
- **Gap Management**: Plan for overnight gaps that hit daily limits
- **Exit Strategy**: Adjust stop losses to account for price limit restrictions
- **Position Sizing**: Reduce sizes due to limited risk management flexibility

### Settlement Period (T+2/T+3)
- **Settlement Risk**: Account for 2-3 day settlement period in planning
- **Loss Calculation**: Pre-calculate if positions will be at loss at settlement
- **Margin Considerations**: Understand margin requirements during settlement
- **Dividend Dates**: Account for ex-dividend dates during settlement period

### Liquidity Factors
- **Volume Requirements**: Minimum average daily volume thresholds
- **Bid-Ask Spreads**: Account for wider spreads in less liquid stocks
- **Order Size Limits**: Understand impact of large orders on price
- **Foreign Room**: Monitor foreign ownership limits for international investors

## Recommendation Structure

### Buy Recommendations
```
BUY [TICKER] at [ENTRY_PRICE]
Target: [TAKE_PROFIT_LEVEL] ([PERCENT_GAIN])
Stop Loss: [STOP_LOSS_LEVEL] ([PERCENT_RISK])
Risk-Reward: [RATIO]:1
Rationale: [TECHNICAL + FUNDAMENTAL REASONS]
Timeline: [EXPECTED_HOLDING_PERIOD]
```

### Sell Recommendations
```
SELL [TICKER] at [ENTRY_PRICE]
Target: [TAKE_PROFIT_LEVEL] ([PERCENT_GAIN])
Stop Loss: [STOP_LOSS_LEVEL] ([PERCENT_RISK])
Risk-Reward: [RATIO]:1
Rationale: [TECHNICAL + FUNDAMENTAL REASONS]
Timeline: [EXPECTED_HOLDING_PERIOD]
```

### Hold/Neutral Recommendations
```
HOLD [TICKER] - No action recommended
Current Price: [PRICE]
Reason: [SPECIFIC_REASONS_FOR_HOLDING]
Watch Level: [LEVEL_TO_MONITOR_FOR_FUTURE_ACTION]
```

## Sector-Based Recommendations

### Banking Sector
- Focus on large-cap banks with strong fundamentals
- Monitor NPL ratios and provisioning levels
- Consider interest rate environment impact
- Watch for regulatory changes affecting profitability

### Real Estate
- Emphasis on developers with land bank visibility
- Monitor government policy changes affecting sector
- Consider financing costs and credit availability
- Evaluate project pipeline and sales execution

### Consumer Goods/Food & Beverage
- Look for brands with pricing power
- Monitor consumer spending trends
- Consider inflation impact on margins
- Evaluate competitive positioning

### Technology/Telecommunications
- Focus on digital transformation beneficiaries
- Monitor government technology policies
- Consider infrastructure development plans
- Evaluate innovation and competitive advantages

### Industrial/Manufacturing
- Emphasis on export-oriented companies
- Monitor global demand trends
- Consider supply chain resilience
- Evaluate operational efficiency improvements

## Risk Management Integration

### Stop Loss Implementation
- **Technical Stops**: Based on support/resistance levels
- **Percentage Stops**: Fixed percentage from entry
- **Time Stops**: Exit if expected move doesn't occur
- **Fundamental Stops**: Deterioration in company fundamentals

### Take Profit Strategy
- **Technical Targets**: Based on chart patterns and measured moves
- **Risk-Reward Targets**: Exit when target risk-reward achieved
- **Trailing Stops**: Protect profits as trends extend
- **Scaling Out**: Partial profit taking at multiple levels

### Portfolio Considerations
- **Sector Allocation**: Maintain diversified sector exposure
- **Correlation Management**: Monitor correlation between positions
- **Concentration Limits**: Avoid excessive concentration in single stocks
- **Market Beta**: Manage overall portfolio sensitivity to market moves

## Quality Control Standards

### Recommendation Validation
- **Multiple Timeframes**: Confirm signal across multiple timeframes
- **Volume Confirmation**: Ensure adequate volume for the trade idea
- **Fundamental Check**: Basic fundamental alignment with technical signal
- **Risk Assessment**: Complete risk analysis before recommendation
- **Alternative Scenarios**: Consider what would invalidate the trade

### Performance Tracking
- **Recommendation Log**: Track all recommendations with outcomes
- **Win Rate Analysis**: Monitor success rate by recommendation type
- **Risk-Reward Achievement**: Track actual vs projected risk-reward
- **Timing Effectiveness**: Evaluate entry/exit timing quality
- **Learning Integration**: Update approach based on results

## Communication Standards

### Clarity Requirements
- **Specific Entry Prices**: Clear, specific entry levels
- **Precise Targets**: Defined take-profit and stop-loss levels
- **Timeline Expectations**: Realistic holding period estimates
- **Risk Disclosure**: Clear statement of potential losses
- **Actionable Language**: Direct, unambiguous instructions

### Format Consistency
- **Standard Template**: Use consistent format for all recommendations
- **Clear Hierarchy**: Most important information first
- **Quantified Elements**: All levels and percentages clearly stated
- **Rationale Explanation**: Reasoning behind each recommendation
- **Follow-up Guidance**: Instructions for post-entry management

## Integration with Other Aspects

### With Risk Assessment
- Incorporate composite risk scores into position sizing
- Adjust recommendations based on overall risk environment
- Include risk warnings with each recommendation
- Align risk-reward targets with risk tolerance

### With Technical Analysis
- Base recommendations on technical setups and patterns
- Include technical rationale in recommendation text
- Monitor technical indicators for position management
- Use technical signals for exit timing

### With Market Regime
- Align recommendation style with current market regime
- Adjust risk parameters based on regime characteristics
- Modify position sizing based on regime stability
- Consider regime change implications for existing positions

## Quality Standards

- Provide specific, actionable recommendations
- Include clear risk-reward parameters
- Consider Vietnamese market characteristics
- Maintain consistency in recommendation format
- Include comprehensive rationale for each recommendation
- Align recommendations with user's risk tolerance and objectives
