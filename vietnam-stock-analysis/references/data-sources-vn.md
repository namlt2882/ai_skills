# Vietnamese Financial Data Sources

Comprehensive reference of reliable data sources for Vietnamese financial markets, including official exchanges, data providers, and research resources.

## Official Exchange Sources

### HOSE (Ho Chi Minh Stock Exchange)
- **Website**: https://www.hsx.vn
- **Data Available**: Real-time quotes, historical data, market statistics, listed company information
- **Key Features**: 
  - Live market data and indices
  - Company announcements and disclosures
  - Market reports and statistics
  - Trading calendar and schedule
- **Access Method**: Web portal with delayed data; real-time feeds available through vendors
- **Coverage**: Over 350 listed companies, primarily large and mid-cap

### HNX (Hanoi Stock Exchange)
- **Website**: https://www.hnx.vn
- **Data Available**: Real-time quotes, historical data, market statistics, derivatives information
- **Key Features**:
  - Live market data for HNX-indexed stocks
  - Bond market information
  - Derivatives market data (futures)
  - SME market information (HNX-Conect)
- **Access Method**: Web portal with delayed data; real-time feeds available through vendors
- **Coverage**: Over 350 listed companies, mid-cap and small-cap focus

### UPCoM (Unlisted Public Company Market)
- **Operator**: Vietnam Securities Depository (VSD)
- **Data Available**: Quotes for unlisted public companies
- **Key Features**:
  - Trading information for non-listed public companies
  - Corporate governance information
  - Shareholding structure data
- **Access Method**: Through VSD and participating securities companies
- **Coverage**: Over 700 companies, primarily smaller enterprises

## Economic and Macro Data Sources

### General Statistics Office of Vietnam (GSO)
- **Website**: https://www.gso.gov.vn
- **Data Coverage**: National economic statistics
- **Key Data**:
  - GDP growth figures
  - Inflation data (CPI)
  - Employment statistics
  - Industrial production
  - Trade balance data
- **Release Schedule**: Monthly and quarterly reports
- **Unique Value**: Official government economic statistics

### State Bank of Vietnam (SBV)
- **Website**: https://sbv.gov.vn
- **Data Coverage**: Monetary policy and banking statistics
- **Key Data**:
  - Interest rates and monetary policy
  - Exchange rates (USD/VND, other currencies)
  - Banking sector statistics
  - Money supply data
- **Release Schedule**: Daily exchange rates, periodic policy updates
- **Unique Value**: Official monetary authority data

### Ministry of Planning and Investment (MPI)
- **Website**: https://www.mpi.gov.vn
- **Data Coverage**: Investment and development statistics
- **Key Data**:
  - Foreign direct investment (FDI) data
  - Investment project approvals
  - Economic development indicators
- **Release Schedule**: Monthly and quarterly reports
- **Unique Value**: Investment flow information

## Data Quality Considerations

### Accuracy Verification
- **Cross-Reference Multiple Sources**: Verify critical data across multiple providers
- **Official Source Priority**: Give preference to exchange and government sources
- **Timeliness Checks**: Ensure data is current and appropriately timestamped
- **Historical Consistency**: Check for data adjustments and corporate actions

### Data Limitations
- **Delayed vs Real-Time**: Understand the difference in data availability
- **Corporate Actions**: Account for stock splits, dividends, and rights issues
- **Market Hours**: Data availability during Vietnamese trading hours
- **Holiday Calendar**: Account for Vietnamese holidays affecting data

## Recommended Data Usage

### For Real-Time Trading
- Primary: DStock (VNDIRECT) for comprehensive real-time data
- Backup: CafeF or VietStock for secondary verification
- News Integration: Combine with real-time news feeds

### For Fundamental Analysis
- Primary: Official exchange websites for company filings
- Financials: DStock, VietStock for financial ratios and data
- Economic Context: GSO and SBV for macro data

### For Research and Screening
- Primary: DStock for advanced screening tools
- Alternative: VietStock for fundamental analysis tools
- Cross-Validation: Use multiple sources for critical decisions

## Data Integration Best Practices

### Source Hierarchy
1. **Official Sources**: Exchange and government data first
2. **Licensed Vendors**: Professional data providers second
3. **Public Sources**: Free sources for supplementary information
4. **Cross-Verification**: Always verify critical data across sources

### Update Frequency
- **Real-Time Data**: During trading hours for active positions
- **Daily Updates**: End-of-day data for position management
- **Weekly Reviews**: Weekly data review for longer-term positions
- **Monthly Deep Dive**: Monthly comprehensive data analysis

### Error Handling
- **Data Validation**: Implement checks for reasonable values
- **Outlier Detection**: Flag unusual data points for review
- **Backup Sources**: Have alternative sources for critical data
- **Documentation**: Record data sources and timestamps

## Compliance and Legal Considerations

### Data Usage Rights
- **License Compliance**: Respect terms of use for each data source
- **Redistribution Rights**: Understand what data can be shared
- **Attribution Requirements**: Properly attribute data sources
- **Commercial Use**: Ensure appropriate licenses for commercial applications

### Regulatory Compliance
- **Market Data Rules**: Follow exchange rules for data usage
- **Insider Trading**: Use only publicly available information
- **Disclosure Requirements**: Understand what information must be disclosed
- **Record Keeping**: Maintain records of data sources and usage
